    Support Core Area — Grounding Context¶

    Dialpad Support is a core Dialpad product. It is one of three primary commercial products in the Dialpad suite — alongside Dialpad Connect (UCaaS) and Dialpad Sell (sales engagement) — and is sold, marketed, and developed as a first-class product line under the Dialpad brand.

    1. What Dialpad Support Is¶

    Dialpad Support is Dialpad's AI-powered CCaaS (Contact Center as a Service) product. It provides omnichannel customer support with voice, digital engagement (chat, SMS, email, social), AI-driven coaching, automated quality management, and deep analytics — all unified with Dialpad Connect (UCaaS) in a single TrueCaaS platform.

    One-liner: AI-powered contact center software with live AI coaching, real-time sentiment tracking, omnichannel engagement, and integrated analytics for customer support teams.

    Strategic outcome: Strong alternative to Nice, Genesys, and Five9.

    2. Product Packaging & Tiers¶

    Dialpad Support Bundles¶

    FY27 Packaging Refresh (In Progress)¶

    Moving from feature-by-feature add-ons to value-based "containers":

    • Live Coach: AI Custom Moments, RTA, real-time suggestions
    • WFM: Forecasting, scheduling, adherence

    Essentials remains limited; Advanced gets QM + Live Coach included; Premium gets everything including WFM.

    3. Feature Inventory¶

    Contact Center Core¶

    • Routing: Longest idle, round robin, fixed order, random order, skills-based routing, queue priority
    • IVR: Advanced multi-level auto attendant with customizable open/closed/holiday hours routing
    • Queues: Hold queues with delay announcements, caller position, exit-to-voicemail, callback requests
    • Agent Management: On-duty/off-duty status, wrap-up time, dispositions, call waiting, agent screen capture
    • Supervisory Tools: Real-time monitoring (listen, barge, takeover), live transcriptions for supervisors, real-time dashboards, status alerts
    • Call Features: Call transfer, call parking (personal lines), manual and automatic recording
    • Capacity: Up to 500 contact centers per account; up to 25 simultaneous ring (expanding to 50+ for enterprise)

    Departments vs. Contact Centers¶

    Departments offer basic routing (longest idle, round robin, fixed order, simultaneous). Contact Centers add: skills-based routing, queue priority, advanced hold queue, real-time dashboards, alerts, CSAT, dispositions, wrap-up time, agent screen capture, and all AI features (RTA, Playbooks, AI CSAT, Scorecards, sentiment, custom moments).

    AI Features (Contact Center Only)¶

    Feature Description
    Real-Time Transcription Live transcription of calls with speaker diarization
    AI Recaps Post-call summaries with action items
    AI CSAT Survey-free inferred customer satisfaction scoring
    AI Scorecards Automated/accelerated QA reviews
    AI Playbooks Real-time adherence tracking for defined call flows
    Real-Time Assist (RTA) Triggered guidance cards during live interactions
    AI Live Coach Real-time coaching overlay with custom moments and suggestions
    Custom Moments Keyword/phrase tracking for compliance and coaching
    Live Call Sentiment Real-time sentiment analysis during calls
    Speech Coaching Talk-to-listen ratio, filler words, talk speed
    AI Voice Isolation Background noise suppression

    Omnichannel / Digital Engagement¶

    • Channels: WebChat, SMS/MMS, Email, Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, WhatsApp
    • Agent Desktop: Unified workspace for voice + digital with shared customer history
    • Agentic AI: GA autonomous voice + chat agents with human handoff
    • Digital Engagement Licensing: One-channel or all-channels add-on

    Analytics¶

    • Canned Reports: Call history, queue health, callbacks, Power Dialer, agent productivity, AI agent analytics
    • Real-Time Dashboard: Live wallboard with queue stats, agent status, SLA metrics
    • Scheduled Reporting: Automated report delivery
    • Playlists: Curated call recording collections for coaching
    • Key Metrics: Wait time, service level, abandon threshold, disposition usage, CSAT results, agent status
    • AI Analytics: Agentic AI performance (deflection rate, success rate, AHT comparison, ROI calculator)

    Workforce Management (WFM)¶

    • Forecasting: Call volume prediction
    • Scheduling: Automated agent schedule generation
    • Adherence Tracking: Real-time adherence monitoring (market-leading capability)
    • Shrinkage Management: Account for breaks, training, admin time
    • FY27 Pivot: Moving from planning-oriented features to real-time workforce optimization — intraday reforecasting, agent redeployment, dynamic response to actual conditions
    • Agentic Integration: WFM calculates how many human agents are needed based on actual AI agent performance
    • Target: $7.6M ARR

    4. FY27 Strategic Priorities¶

    Overarching Vision¶

    Treat Support + Analytics + AI as a single, cohesive platform investment — not a collection of disconnected features. Customers evaluate Support as an operational platform, not individual features.

    Four Strategic Pillars¶

    Pillar 1: Scalable Contact Center Foundations¶

    • Advanced multi-skill routing model (replace simplistic skills-based routing that creates CC explosion)
    • Expand phone number limit per CC
    • Skill-based routing improvements
    • More simultaneous ring capacity
    • Merged identity: single customer profile across voice, SMS, email, chat channels
    • Scheduled callbacks improvements

    Pillar 2: Unified, Actionable Analytics¶

    • Unified analytics with consistent definitions across voice and digital
    • Design analytics for personas (agent, supervisor, executive), not as raw data dumps
    • Agentic AI observability: closed feedback loop where insights drive improvement
    • Cross-channel AI insights

    Pillar 3: Trustworthy, Global AI¶

    • PII redaction GA across transcripts and audio with full admin controls
    • Multilingual AI parity: 10+ languages with automatic language detection
    • AI CSAT expansion to outbound, direct numbers, and digital interactions
    • AI insight that surfaces conversation trends and root causes

    Pillar 4: Innovation with AI¶

    • LLM-powered scorecards
    • Prescriptive recommended actions from AI insights
    • Agentic AI analytics (deflection rate, success rate, AHT benchmarks, ROI)

    Q4 FY26 Win/Loss (Contact Center)¶

    • Average lost deal is 2–3x the won deal — enterprise gap is real
    • Product-controllable loss themes: concurrent licensing, predictive dialer depth, MS Teams integration, BPO multi-tenancy, omnichannel email queueing

    Thesis: "We win and retain on value, not feature parity; customers who see better quality and efficiency outcomes are stickier and expand more."

    7. Competitive Context (Support-Specific)¶

    Competitor Threat Support-Specific Notes
    Genesys 🔴 High Enterprise depth, WEM suite, integration strength (Salesforce+ServiceNow), token-based AI pricing
    NICE CXone 🔴 High Breadth (WFM, QM, recording), Enlighten AI suite, Cognigy acquisition for agentic
    Five9 🟠 Medium Strong outbound/dialer, analyst recognition, lacks simultaneous ring
    Talkdesk 🟠 Medium Digital engagement, industry packages
    Zoom 🟠 Medium Price competitive, grabbing CCaaS churners
    Nextiva 🟠 Medium Enterprise push, winning some table-stakes RFPs

    Where Support Wins¶

    • AI native and included in base (not token-based or add-on)
    • Unified UCaaS+CCaaS (TrueCaaS)
    • Real-time coaching during live calls
    • Faster deployment (2 months vs. 12 months with Genesys)
    • AI CSAT (survey-free) — unique differentiator

    8. Agentic AI in Support Context¶

    Agentic AI is both a standalone product and a capability within Support:

    • AI Agents handle the majority of support requests; humans focus on edge cases
    • Human transfer is positioned as a trust-building feature, not a failure
    • Agentic AI Agents escalate to human agents on the same platform seamlessly
    • WFM calculates human agent needs based on actual AI agent performance
    • Analytics track AI vs. human performance side-by-side (deflection, FCR, AHT, CSAT)

    Key Agentic Metrics¶

    Metric Description
    Deflection rate Contained vs. escalated interactions
    Success rate Deflection × CSAT
    AI AHT vs. Human AHT Average handle time comparison
    Skills usage Skill gap identification
    ROI calculator Agent hours saved, cost per interaction
