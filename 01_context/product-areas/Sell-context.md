# Sell Core Area — Grounding Context

> **Dialpad Sell is a core Dialpad product.** It is one of three primary commercial products in the Dialpad suite — alongside Dialpad Connect (UCaaS) and Dialpad Support (CCaaS) — and is sold, marketed, and developed as a first-class product line under the Dialpad brand.

## 1. What Sell Is

Dialpad Sell is Dialpad's AI-powered sales engagement product, focused on helping revenue teams run high-volume, high-quality, communication-led workflows across outbound calling, live coaching, and team performance.

At its core, Sell owns the **communication-centric parts of the sales workflow** — not the full CRM, contact database, or end-to-end marketing automation stack.

Sell is built on the broader Dialpad platform (UCaaS + CCaaS, shared AI and data layer), so its value comes not just from point features but from how calling, conversation intelligence, coaching, and analytics work together.

**Strategic outcome:** Credible alternative to Gong.

---

## 2. Core Scope

Sell is centered on four connected product surfaces:

| Surface | Role |
|---|---|
| **Power Dialer** | Outbound dialing and campaign-based engagement at scale |
| **Playbooks** | Structured guidance and adherence tracking during conversations |
| **RTA (Real-Time Assist)** | In-call prompts and live assistance while the conversation is happening |
| **Performance Management** | Analytics and coaching views that help managers understand rep effectiveness and guide improvement |

These areas are interdependent: Power Dialer increases outbound activity, Playbooks and RTA improve call execution in the moment, and Performance Management turns conversation data into coaching and operational insight.

---

## 3. Product Philosophy

Sell is a **communication-led sales engagement layer**. The product is strongest when it helps teams place more calls, have better conversations, and turn live interactions into actionable insight for reps and managers.

**Intentionally in scope:**
- Scaling outbound engagement
- Improving conversation quality in real time
- Making account and team context more actionable
- Exposing analytics that help teams understand what is working and what needs coaching

**Intentionally out of scope:**
- Replacing systems of record (CRM, contact databases)
- Building broad workflow ownership across the entire sales stack
- Feature-for-feature competition with Gong, ZoomInfo, or Outreach on contact management or cadence automation

---

## 4. Power Dialer

Power Dialer is the outbound execution engine inside Sell — connecting live calls to agents at scale from large pools of dialable contacts and campaign workflows.

**Core responsibilities:**
- Campaign creation and management
- List and contact handling
- Scaling outbound voice engagement
- Expanding toward multi-touch and multi-channel workflows
- Creating visibility into campaign outcomes inside analytics

Power Dialer also acts as a bridge between classic outbound workflows and newer automation patterns, including agentic outbound use cases and tighter campaign orchestration across channels.

> **FY27 priority:** Power Dialer GA is a Q1 non-negotiable. This is the most critical near-term Sell deliverable.

---

## 5. Playbooks

Playbooks map desired talk tracks, qualification frameworks, or process steps into the live conversation experience — providing structured call guidance and making adherence visible during and after calls.

Their job is not just to display guidance, but to make adherence **measurable** so reps, managers, and analytics surfaces can understand how well conversations followed the intended approach.

Playbooks become most valuable when connected to RTA, summaries, analytics, and team coaching workflows — not when operating as a standalone checklist.

---

## 6. RTA (Real-Time Assist)

RTA is the in-call assist layer that surfaces guidance during live interactions. Its role is to reduce the gap between what a rep should do and what they are actually doing in the moment — through prompts, suggestions, and context-aware assistance while the conversation is still happening.

RTA sits alongside Playbooks as part of the real-time coaching experience. Current direction points toward a **simpler, more unified assistant-style experience** rather than many fragmented coaching surfaces.

---

## 7. Performance Management

Performance Management helps managers move from individual calls to repeatable coaching and team-level improvement. It depends on conversation data, live coaching signals, and analytics to answer:

- How are reps performing?
- Where is coaching most needed?
- Which behaviors correlate with stronger outcomes?
- How does team effectiveness change over time?

This area is not only about reporting — it is about turning raw conversation intelligence into **manager workflows**: review, compare, coach, and improve.

---

## 8. How the Four Areas Work Together

These four surfaces should be read as one connected system:

```
Power Dialer  →  creates more sales conversations
Playbooks     →  defines what good execution looks like in those conversations
RTA           →  helps reps execute better while the call is happening
Perf. Mgmt    →  helps managers review patterns across calls and coach the team
```

Sell is not a dialer plus AI add-ons. It is an **outbound and coaching system** built around live conversations, guided execution, and manager visibility.

---

## 9. Boundaries with Adjacent Areas

| Adjacent area | How Sell relates | What Sell does not own |
|---|---|---|
| **Dialpad Platform (Connect)** | Relies on shared calling, transcription, and conversation data | Core telephony infrastructure, messaging fabric, admin UX |
| **Shared Services (Analytics)** | Benefits from shared analytics platform for reporting consistency | Analytics platform infrastructure, cross-product data layer |
| **Ecosystem (CRM)** | Connects to CRM context (SFDC, HubSpot) for account enrichment | CRM system of record, contact database, sequencing automation |
| **Agentic AI Platform** | Intersects on outbound automation and proactive assistance | Autonomous agent infrastructure, agent studio, orchestration |

This boundary is important because it keeps Sell focused on the part of the workflow where Dialpad is most differentiated: **the live communication layer and the intelligence generated from it**.
