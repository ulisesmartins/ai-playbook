# Dialpad — Mission, Strategic Priorities & Direction

## 1. What Dialpad Is

Dialpad is an **AI-native, unified communications platform** combining UCaaS + CCaaS in a single app with a shared AI and data layer. The platform unifies phones, messaging, video (Meetings), contact center, and digital channels — powered by a proprietary AI stack that includes GA Agentic AI.

**One-liner:** Dialpad is the AI-powered customer communications platform that connects humans and AI agents together across voice, video, messaging, and digital channels.

### Proprietary AI Foundation

- 10 billion+ voice minutes analyzed since 2016
- Enterprise-grade AI: native, real-time, dual-cloud architecture
- Proprietary dataset trained on business conversations — speaks the language of business
- Focus shifted from AI Research toward **Applied AI** — mixing 3rd-party models, fine-tuning, and optimizing the orchestration layer rather than training proprietary models from scratch

## 4. FY27 Strategic Priorities (Calendar Year 2026)

### Company-Level Imperatives

1. **Agentic AI as the core revenue driver** — AI Agents handle the majority of customer support requests; humans focus on edge cases.
2. **Enterprise readiness & reliability** — platform must feel predictable, explainable, and scalable. Reliability is the #1 priority across telephony, infrastructure, and product.
3. **Best onboarding & support** — modernize tooling (CSR, CDP, ES) and enable partner-led growth.
4. **Monthly innovation cadence** — ship meaningful, production-ready improvements on the first Thursday of every month ("First Thursday" rhythm).

### Per-Area FY27 Strategic Focus

#### Agentic AI Platform
- Autonomous voice + chat agents that answer, ask, and act
- Agent Studio for no-code agent building with visual workflows
- Hybrid journeys: mix deterministic IVR logic with agentic behavior
- Optimize & test framework for agent certification
- Target: feature parity with all major competitors by Q2 2026, then focus on operational excellence and revenue growth

#### Dialpad Support
- Treat Support + Analytics + AI as a single cohesive platform investment
- Unified analytics with consistent definitions across surfaces
- AI trust: PII redaction GA, multilingual parity (10+ languages), automatic language detection
- Skill-based routing, merged identity across channels, scheduled callbacks

#### Dialpad Sell
- Power Dialer GA (Q1 non-negotiable) — scale outbound engagement
- Account Hub — single pane of glass for account insights combining conversational data + CRM context
- AI uplift for Playbooks, RTA, and Assistant — real-time coaching and automation
- Not aiming to replace Gong/ZoomInfo/Outreach; instead own the communication-centric parts of the workflow

#### Omnichannel
- Unified agent + supervisor experience across voice and digital
- Merged identity: single customer profile across phone, SMS, email, chat
- Channel simplification: direct WhatsApp integration (remove 3rd-party layer)
- Unified application build experience: one builder for deterministic + agentic journeys
- AI parity with voice across all digital channels

#### Dialpad Connect
- Intelligent Admin: universal search + agentic AI for settings management
- Contact management improvements and shared line management
- Granular RBAC for enterprise delegation
- Deep Summaries GA and read-friendly transcriptions
- Enterprise customer commits to unblock large deals

#### Telephony & Carrier Platform
- Multi-region infrastructure availability (no single-region failure)
- Full containerization for faster recovery and observability
- Spam mitigation and outbound number reputation
- Twilio integration for unified SMS/messaging
- TLS encryption and PCI compliance
- BYOC (Bring Your Own Carrier) stabilization

#### Meetings
- Performance & reliability as the top priority
- AI feature parity with core Dialpad (Deep Summaries, Playbooks, AI CSAT)
- Enterprise-ready: unified settings, RBAC, room systems
- Not competing feature-for-feature with Zoom/Teams/Meet — differentiate through unified platform

#### Shared Services (Billing, Growth, Analytics Platform)
- Billing: NetSuite invoice visibility, usage-based billing for Agentic, Stripe migration evaluation
- Growth: PLG workflows, self-service monetization
- Analytics Platform: scalable, unified analytics serving all Core Areas
- Philosophy: expose services that unblock other teams — minimal Shared Services team involvement required

#### Admin & Partner Portals
- Agentic-first: AI for ops and admin automation
- CDP (Channel Dealer Portal) rollout for partner-led deployments
- Bulk capabilities for enterprise-scale operations
- Information architecture improvements

#### WFM
- Pivot from planning-oriented features to real-time workforce optimization
- Intraday reforecasting and agent redeployment
- Native integration with Dialpad Support for human agent orchestration alongside AI agents

## 5. Competitive Positioning

### Where Dialpad Wins

- **AI-native platform** — AI is built into the core, not bolted on. Works out of the box.
- **Unified UCaaS + CCaaS** — single app, shared AI/data layer, one vendor
- **Real-time AI** — live coaching, live transcription, live sentiment, live suggestions
- **Dual-cloud architecture** — secure, reliable, compliant, scalable
- **Execution cadence** — monthly First Thursday releases, not quarterly/annual launches
- **Agentic AI with full CCaaS stack** — uniquely positioned to handle both AI and human interactions in one platform

### Competitive Landscape

| Competitor Space | Key Players | Dialpad Differentiation |
|---|---|---|
| CCaaS | Nice, Genesys, Five9 | AI-native vs. AI-bolted-on; unified UCaaS+CCaaS |
| UCaaS | RingCentral, Zoom, Microsoft Teams | AI depth; single-platform approach |
| Sales Engagement | Gong, ZoomInfo, Outreach | Real-time communication + AI; not replacing full CRM/funnel tools |
| Agentic AI | Conversational AI startups | End-to-end CCaaS integration; own the stack; voice + digital |

### What Dialpad Is NOT Trying to Be

- Not a full CRM or marketing automation platform
- Not building contact mining or full-funnel management
- Not competing on visual UI polish over functional depth (FY27)
- Instead: interoperate and integrate with ecosystem partners while owning the communication-centric parts

## 6. Operating Philosophy

### Innovation Model
> "At Dialpad, innovation isn't something we announce once or twice a year. On the first Thursday of every month, we ship meaningful product improvements that customers can use immediately."

### Shared Services Philosophy
> "The core philosophy of Shared Services is to expose services that unblock other teams. If a feature needs billing, the billing team gives the other team all the levers they need, requiring minimal billing team involvement."

### Applied AI Approach
> "The approach shifted from AI Research toward Applied AI, where AI has been commoditized and the key to success lies in understanding how to build applications with off-the-shelf technologies, staying close to customers and design partners, and being able to pivot quickly."

### Enterprise Trust Framework (WALK)
- **W**in with trust — deeply understand customer challenges
- **A**ctivate success — dedicated team for immediate and long-term success
- **L**isten deeply — turn customer feedback into innovation
- **K**eep growing together — scale with evolving business needs
