    Dialpad — Competitive Positioning¶

    1. Competitive Framing Philosophy¶

    Dialpad wins on execution cadence + operational maturity, not on one-off features. Avoid feature-by-feature comparisons. Frame every competitive conversation around value delivery: who we are, what we're good at, and why customers succeed with us.

    "Most vendors in this space innovate in batches. Dialpad innovates continuously."

    How to Win Competitive Conversations¶

    • Lead with outcomes, not features — "What changed for your business?"
    • Highlight First Thursday monthly release cadence vs. competitors' quarterly/annual cycles
    • Position Dialpad as a platform that gets better every month, not a static product
    • When pressed on feature gaps, redirect to total platform value and AI depth

    2. Dialpad Differentiators¶

    Core Platform Advantages¶

    Differentiator What It Means Why Competitors Can't Match
    TrueCaaS Truly unified UCaaS + CCaaS + AI in one app Competitors bolt together separate products; Dialpad shares one architecture, one data layer
    AI-Native AI built into the core — works out of the box, included in base plans Competitors charge add-ons, use 3rd-party AI, or require separate setup
    Real-Time AI Live transcription, coaching, sentiment, suggestions during calls Most competitors offer post-call only or limited real-time capabilities
    Agentic AI + Full CCaaS Autonomous voice + chat agents integrated with human agent workflows Agentic startups lack CCaaS; legacy CCaaS vendors bolt on agentic
    Dual-Cloud Architecture Secure, reliable, compliant, scalable infrastructure Single-cloud vendors face single points of failure
    First Thursday Cadence Production-ready improvements shipped monthly Competitors save innovation for quarterly launches or annual events
    Applied AI Approach Mix best-of-breed models, fine-tune, optimize orchestration layer Competitors either lock into single LLM vendors or train from scratch

    Per-LOB Differentiators¶

    Agentic AI¶

    • Full control over stack and orchestration chain
    • Human transfer as a feature (trust-building), not a failure
    • Verticalized launch motion with design partner–driven iteration
    • Hybrid journeys: deterministic IVR + agentic in one builder
    • Proprietary model (Dialpad A1) to balance cost, performance, and speed

    Support (CCaaS)¶

    • AI is native and included — not an add-on or token-based
    • Unified analytics across voice + digital with consistent definitions
    • AI CSAT (survey-free inferred satisfaction) — unique in market
    • Playbooks + RTA + Scorecards as an integrated coaching engine
    • Agentic AI Agents that escalate to human agents on the same platform

    Sell¶

    • Communication-led engagement — not trying to be a CRM or contact database
    • Power Dialer integrated into the CCaaS analytics ecosystem (competitors use standalone tools)
    • Account Hub combining conversational data + CRM context
    • Real-time AI coaching during live calls

    Connect (UCaaS)¶

    • Unified UC + CC: "Customers who need unified UC & CC prefer Dialpad" (Forrester 2025)
    • "Customer Favorite" label — one of only two vendors in Forrester 2025 UC Wave
    • AI transcription, summaries, and action items included in base plan
    • Intelligent Admin with agentic AI for settings management

    Integrations & Ecosystem¶

    • High flexibility: APIs, iPaaS (Membrane), workflows, SDKs
    • Strong backbone for rapidly expanding agentic offering
    • Simpler UX than competitors for integration configuration

    3. Competitive Landscape by Category¶

    UCaaS & Video¶

    Competitor Threat Level Strengths Dialpad Advantage
    RingCentral 🔴 High Market share, international presence, integration breadth AI depth, unified UC+CC, First Thursday cadence
    Zoom 🟠 Medium Brand recognition, video dominance, 3000+ marketplace apps Native AI (not bolted-on), TrueCaaS, contact center depth
    Microsoft Teams 🟠 Medium Enterprise embed via M365, Copilot integration Purpose-built for business comms, superior telephony, AI CSAT/Scorecards
    8x8 🟢 Low XCaaS combined offering AI maturity, product polish, Agentic AI
    Nextiva 🟠 Medium Simplified UCaaS, enterprise push AI depth, CCaaS maturity

    Other UCaaS: Cisco Webex, GoToConnect, Vonage, Sangoma, Wildix, Intermedia Unite, Aircall

    CCaaS Core¶

    Competitor Threat Level Strengths Dialpad Advantage
    Genesys 🔴 High Enterprise depth, analyst recognition, WEM suite, $1.5B from Salesforce/ServiceNow Unified UCaaS+CCaaS, AI included in base (not token-based), faster innovation cycle
    NICE CXone 🔴 High Breadth (WFM, QM, recording), Enlighten AI suite, Cognigy acquisition Unified platform, simpler pricing, Applied AI approach
    Five9 🟠 Medium Strong outbound/dialer, analyst recognition TrueCaaS, native AI, Power Dialer closing the gap
    Talkdesk 🟠 Medium Digital engagement, industry packages AI depth, unified UC+CC, Agentic maturity
    AWS Connect 🟠 Medium Pay-per-use, AWS ecosystem lock-in Turnkey platform vs. build-it-yourself, included AI

    Other CCaaS: Cisco Webex CC, 8x8 XCaaS, Content Guru, Vonage CCaaS, Avaya

    Sales Engagement¶

    Competitor Threat Level Strengths Dialpad Advantage
    Gong 🟠 Medium Revenue intelligence, conversation analytics depth Real-time communication platform (not just recording), native telephony
    Outreach / SalesLoft 🟢 Low Sales cadence automation, multi-channel sequencing Own the communication layer; interoperate on cadence
    ZoomInfo 🟢 Low Contact database, intent data Not competing here; integrate instead

    Agentic AI / Virtual Agents¶

    Competitor Threat Level Strengths Dialpad Advantage
    NICE Autopilot (Cognigy) 🟠 Medium End-to-end journey, enterprise features Rough waters since NICE acquisition; Dialpad has full CCaaS integration
    Genesys AI 🟠 Medium Routing + bots in one platform Dialpad's unified UC+CC+Agentic approach
    Talkdesk Virtual Agent 🟢 Low GenAI capabilities Less mature than Dialpad's GA Agentic
    Zoom Virtual Agent 🟢 Low Solvvy acquisition Narrow scope, no full CCaaS
    Agentic startups 🟠 Medium Vertical depth, fast iteration No customer base, no CCaaS, piecemeal solution
    RingCentral 🟢 Low AI Receptionist Lacks holistic AI product strategy

    Digital-only bots: Intercom (Fin), Ada, Zendesk AI, Freshworks Freddy, Forethought, Kustomer IQ, Netomi, Sprinklr, LivePerson, PolyAI

    Voice+chat hybrids: Kore.ai, Cognigy.AI, Boost.ai, Google CCAI, Yellow.ai, IBM watsonx, Microsoft Copilot Studio, SoundHound AI (Amelia), Omilia

    Integrations¶

    Competitor Threat Level Strengths Dialpad Advantage
    Genesys / Amazon Connect / Five9 🔴 High Analyst-recognized integration strength Catching up; iPaaS (Membrane) backbone accelerating breadth
    Aircall 🟠 Medium HubSpot integration depth, "insights card" Rest of CCaaS offering is behind Dialpad
    Zoom 🟢 Low 3000+ marketplace apps Marketplace breadth is surface-level; Dialpad has deeper AI integration
    Agentic startups 🟠 Medium Vertical-specific integrations (EHRs, ATS) Single-vertical focus limits scale; Dialpad covers multiple verticals
