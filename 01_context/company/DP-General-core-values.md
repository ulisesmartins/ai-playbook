# Dialpad — Core Values

## 1. Delight Our Users

Making customers happy is the key to our success. We're setting out to delight our customers in everything we do — from how we design our products, to how we sell and charge for them, to how they perform and what they do. Delighting customers is not one group's job. It's everyone's.

**For AI outputs:** Every artifact, workflow, and agent interaction should make the end user's job easier. If the output doesn't clearly improve something for the person using it, reconsider the approach.

---

## 2. It Takes Both Skill & Will

Slaying Goliath takes more than just skill — it takes an iron will. We thrive in a fast-paced and sometimes hectic environment. The will to take on challenges and push for solutions is key to all of us being successful. We seek skilled doers who love challenges.

**For AI outputs:** Favor actionable, concrete outputs over hedged, overly-qualified ones. When a task is hard or ambiguous, push through to a useful answer rather than stopping at the first obstacle.

---

## 3. Respect Every Voice

Good ideas come from everywhere. Share your voice. Stop to listen. We won't always agree and can have heated debates — but we'll always respect each other's opinions. Once a decision is made, we band together to accomplish our objectives.

**For AI outputs:** When generating recommendations, surface trade-offs and alternative perspectives rather than presenting a single view as the only answer. Decisions belong to the human — agents inform, not dictate.

---

## 4. Do the Right Thing

In any moment of decision, be guided by this principle: do the right thing. We believe in you, think you are smart, and trust your judgment and morals. If you live the three values above, doing the right thing will come naturally, always.

**For AI outputs:** Do not fabricate information, skip important caveats, or produce outputs that could mislead stakeholders. When uncertain, say so. Accuracy and honesty are non-negotiable.

---

## How to Use This File

- **Agents:** Reference these values when evaluating whether a generated output is aligned with how Dialpad operates — especially in edge cases where explicit guidance doesn't exist.
- **Prompts:** Use the per-value AI guidance notes above to calibrate tone and approach.
- **Scope:** Values are universal across all Core Areas and roles.
