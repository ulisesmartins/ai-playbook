# Dialpad — Buyer Personas

## Purpose

Defines the economic buyer and influencer personas Dialpad's sales and GTM motion targets. Use when generating competitive analysis, positioning docs, GTM messaging, or pricing/packaging rationale.

> Source: [Dialpad Upmarket ICP & Buyer Personas](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/17yd71_HIrVtLnoKNpCZc1kbhwSrKZWRMNuSoC7oZdzo) | [Understanding Our Buyers — Highspot](https://dialpad.highspot.com/items/65fb4a1dda2e8b21ae079e56)

---

## 1. IT / Technology Buyer

**Title examples:** IT Director, VP of IT, CTO, Director of Technology

**Segment:** Mid-market to Enterprise

**What they care about:**
- Reliability and uptime — SLAs, redundancy, failover
- Security and compliance — SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI, GDPR, SSO, RBAC
- Integration depth — APIs, webhooks, CRM sync, Microsoft Teams compatibility
- Ease of administration — bulk management, provisioning, number management
- Total Cost of Ownership — licensing simplicity, avoiding hidden fees

**Pain points with incumbents:**
- Genesys/NICE are expensive and slow to deploy (12+ months)
- RingCentral has integration gaps and pricing complexity
- Microsoft Teams lacks CCaaS depth and real-time AI coaching

**Dialpad pitch angle:**
- AI-native platform that works out of the box — no add-on required
- Dual-cloud architecture for reliability (enterprise-grade, multi-region)
- Unified UCaaS + CCaaS eliminates the need for two vendors
- Faster deployment: 2 months vs. 12 with legacy CCaaS

## 2. Contact Center / CX Buyer

**Title examples:** VP Customer Experience, Director of Contact Center Operations, Head of CX, Customer Support Director

**Segment:** Mid-market to Enterprise

**What they care about:**
- Agent productivity — reduce AHT, improve first call resolution
- Quality management — CSAT, scorecard automation, coaching at scale
- AI capability — real-time coaching, transcription, sentiment, AI CSAT
- Omnichannel — voice + digital (chat, email, SMS, WhatsApp) in one platform
- Workforce management — forecasting, scheduling, adherence
- Analytics — real-time dashboards, historical reporting, agent leaderboards

**Pain points with incumbents:**
- AI CSAT and Scorecards are token-based add-ons (Genesys, NICE) vs. Dialpad's included
- Manual QA on 3–5% of calls; AI-powered QA on 100%
- Fragmented platforms: separate WFM, QM, telephony vendors

**Dialpad pitch angle:**
- AI CSAT on 100% of calls — survey-free, included in Advanced+
- Unified coaching: Playbooks + RTA + Scorecards as an integrated engine
- Faster time-to-value: supervisors are productive in days, not months

## 3. Sales / Revenue Buyer

**Title examples:** VP Sales, Director of Inside Sales, Head of Sales Operations, Chief Revenue Officer

**Segment:** Mid-market to Enterprise (Sell product)

**What they care about:**
- Rep productivity — more conversations per hour, power dialer
- Real-time coaching — live playbook adherence, RTA during calls
- Conversation intelligence — call recording, summary, trend analysis
- CRM integration — automatic logging, contact sync (SFDC, HubSpot)
- Performance tracking — rep leaderboards, call analytics, coaching reports

**Pain points with incumbents:**
- Gong is post-call only — no real-time AI coaching during live calls
- Separate dialer tools (Outreach, SalesLoft) don't share AI context
- Manual CRM logging wastes rep time

**Dialpad pitch angle:**
- Real-time AI during live calls — not just post-call recording
- Power Dialer natively integrated with analytics and CRM
- Communication-led engagement: own the call layer without replacing the full CRM stack

---

## 4. Finance / Billing Buyer

**Title examples:** CFO, Finance Director, Billing Administrator, Accounts Payable Manager

**Segment:** Mid-market to Enterprise

**What they care about:**
- Invoice clarity — itemized, auditable charges per license and add-on
- Credit management — predictable spend, low balance alerts, auto-recharge controls
- Cost attribution — department or office-level cost allocation
- Usage transparency — distinguish AI agent costs from telephony costs
- Compliance — invoice accuracy for ERP integration (NetSuite, SAP)

**Pain points:**
- Single-pool credits make it hard to separate telephony vs. AI agent spend
- Legacy invoices hide parent-child license relationships
- Manual reconciliation via spreadsheet downloads

**Dialpad pitch angle:**
- Isolated credit wallets: Calling Credit, AI Agent Credit, Dialpad Credits
- Structured invoice: User Licenses → Standalone Add-ons → Nested Add-ons
- Real-time usage dashboard with AI-powered natural language queries

## 5. Buyer Influence Map

| Persona | Decision Authority | Evaluation Role | Deal Influence |
|---|---|---|---|
| IT / Technology | Economic buyer for CC deals | Validates security, reliability, integration | High — can veto |
| CX / Contact Center | Economic buyer or co-buyer | Validates AI, coaching, omnichannel | High — primary champion |
| Sales / Revenue | Economic buyer for Sell product | Validates dialer, CRM, coaching | High — primary champion |
| Finance / Billing | Influencer | Validates pricing structure, invoice clarity | Medium — can delay |
| Operations | Influencer | Validates WFM, scheduling, adherence | Medium |
