# Tools and Software for Research

We have the following tools and software available to perform User Research. They can be used for both getting a list of users / creating an audience and contacting them.

| Tool | What can I find there? | Steward | Links |
|------|------------------------|---------|-------|
| **[Amplitude](http://amplitude.com)** | Event-based tracking in clients. Android, iOS, and Dialpad Everywhere. Every new product should have a tag for tracking. | Data team | [Taxonomy](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1TrvFMtBjPKpns2YzxeiK_qKBe3SiQ3rdtplm3CveQBY/edit) |
| **Appointment Slots** | Google Calendar feature replacing Calendly. External users can book a slot in your calendar. | N/A | --- |
| **Chameleon** *(license required)* | Create campaigns and send in-app messages to audiences, inviting users to interviews. | Growth Marketing | --- |
| **Clari Lost Report** | Weekly email report with churned customers due to product limitations and estimated lost deals. | PMs | Email inbox |
| **Competitive Intelligence (CI)** | Monthly report summarizing the competitive landscape: news, updates, and deep dives on competitors. | PMs | [CI Wiki](https://dialpad.atlassian.net/wiki/x/64FPMw) |
| **CSR** | Internal tool for managing Dialpad accounts and settings. Find CSMs assigned to companies. | IT | [CSR Database](https://dialpad.com/csr) |
| **CSM Team** | Customer Success team --- account managers for Enterprise accounts. | CSM | --- |
| **Dialpad Channels** | App feedback from users via Google Form, captured in `#feedback-from-users` channels. | PMs | [Feedback spreadsheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1w9RnBKlGra3H1RGI09po3da5VCLIGe-EioI9_yofz3M/edit) |
| **[Dovetail](https://teamsurfboard.dovetail.com/start/)** | Research repository --- wiki for user research insights and learnings. AI features help find insights on specific topics. | UI | --- |
| **Experimentation / A/B Tests** | Repository for user testing experiments run by the Growth team. | Growth Marketing | --- |
| **[Highspot](https://dialpad.highspot.com/)** | Dialpad resource hub with information used mostly by Sales and Rev orgs. | Marketing | --- |
| **Marketo** | Stores user data from SFDC. Creates email, newsletter, and other campaigns for contacting users at scale. | MarkOps team | [Marketo fields](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1wzL2MBsG9_QEGUh0M7ttN_sUNv8MHi2HPDcb9psXxTc/edit) |
| **[Google BigQuery](https://console.cloud.google.com/bigquery)** *(special permissions required)* | Primary database where all Dialpad info is stored. Requires SQL knowledge. | ENG | --- |
| **Segment** *(license required)* | Gathers data from many sources (Amplitude, GBQ, etc.). Create audiences and contact users through other platforms. | Growth Marketing | [Segment CDP](https://dialpad.atlassian.net/wiki/display/MAR/Segment+Customer+Data+Platform) |
| **PMs Monthly Metric** | Monthly call where PMs share Adoption, Usage, and Revenue performance for each product area. | PMs | [Meeting notes](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1hemD1MfHOmIL6gmpJRV6dqCgFBJM6CDb3fg3pDrCrTI/edit) |
| **[Secoda AI](https://app.secoda.co/)** | Fed with internal data sources, works as an LLM --- ask questions and get query-backed answers. | Data team | --- |
| **[Tableau](https://10ay.online.tableau.com/#/site/dialpad/home)** | Data visualization tool with dashboards from Google BigQuery. | Data team | [User View Dataset](https://10ay.online.tableau.com/#/site/dialpad/datasources/76829466/connections) |
| **[UserVoice](https://dialpad.uservoice.com/)** | Platform managed by CS team to gather product/feature ideas submitted by customers. | Customer Success | --- |
| **[Wootric](https://dashboard.wootric.com/)** | Captures NPS feedback from users. Request access through Okta. | PMs | --- |

!!! warning "Before contacting users"

    Always review the Customer and Partner Exception List and contact their CSMs if they are listed there.
