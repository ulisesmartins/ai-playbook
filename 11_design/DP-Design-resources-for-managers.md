# Resources for Design Managers

The following section centralizes assets that are shared in different sections of the Design Confluence site. Here is a toolkit for Design Managers.

| Asset | Purpose / Summary |
|-------|-------------------|
| [Dialpad Managers Confluence](https://dialpad.atlassian.net/wiki/display/MGR) | Confluence site managed by People Ops — provides a range of useful resources for Dialpad Managers |
| [Visualizing Design Work](https://dialpad.atlassian.net/wiki/x/EAD4WQ) | A guide to how we visualize Design work using our project management tools |
| [Critiques and Design Reviews](https://dialpad.atlassian.net/wiki/x/D4AiKg) | Guidelines for critiques and design reviews |
| [Design Leadership Reviews](https://dialpad.atlassian.net/wiki/x/coDaIw) | A guide for how we handle Design Reviews with DLT, plus a tracker of those reviews |

| [New Hire Onboarding Guide](https://dialpad.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AQAaFQ) | Step-by-step guide after the Recruiting team has extended an offer and before the new hire joins |
| [Various Documents & Templates](https://dialpad.atlassian.net/wiki/x/5ILzMQ) | A list of various documents to help you and designers expedite certain processes |
